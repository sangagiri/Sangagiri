About Sanagiri

Sanagiri, popularly known as Sankari, is a historic town located in Salem District, Tamil Nadu. The town derives its name from the Sankaréswarar Shiva temple, a revered ancient shrine that has stood for centuries.

Nestled along the Salem–Coimbatore National Highway (NH-544), Sanagiri is strategically connected to major cities while preserving its unique cultural identity. The town is internationally recognized for its exquisite silk and cotton handloom sarees — the celebrated "Sankari Sarees" — which are cherished across Tamil Nadu and beyond.

The town's vibrant weaving community, lush agricultural landscape, and deeply rooted traditions make Sanagiri a proud representative of Tamil Nadu's rich cultural tapestry.
