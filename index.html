History & Heritage

About Sanagiri

Sanagiri — popularly known as Sankari — is a historic town in Salem District, Tamil Nadu. The name itself is sacred: Sankara + Giri, meaning the hill of Lord Shankara (Shiva), rooted in the ancient Sankareeswarar temple at its heart.

Nestled along the Salem–Coimbatore National Highway (NH-544), Sanagiri is internationally celebrated for its exquisite handloom silk and cotton sarees — the renowned 'Sankari Sarees' — cherished across Tamil Nadu, gifted at weddings and festivals alike.

Beyond the looms, Sanagiri is a town of deep traditions — ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and a warm community that has preserved its cultural identity through generations.
